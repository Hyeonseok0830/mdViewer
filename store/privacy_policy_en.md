# MdPad Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

---

MdPad ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

---

## 1. Personal Data MdPad Collects

**MdPad does not collect any personal data.**

The App works as follows:

- It only reads local files and folders that you explicitly select.
- All data is processed entirely on your device.
- No data is ever transmitted to external servers.
- No account creation, login, or registration is required.

### Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores only the following settings locally to improve your experience:

| Item | Storage Location | Purpose |
|------|-----------------|---------|
| Theme preference (light/dark) | App local storage | Restore theme on next launch |
| Editor mode preference | App local storage | Restore mode on next launch |

This information cannot be used to identify you personally and is never transmitted externally.

---

## 2. How MdPad Uses Personal Data

MdPad does not collect personal data, so no collected data is used or shared.

The App uses only on-device information for the following purposes:

- **Providing app features**: Markdown file rendering, editing, and preview
- **Preserving user preferences**: Restoring theme and editor mode settings

---

## 3. Reasons MdPad Discloses Personal Data

MdPad does not disclose or sell any personal data to third parties.

---

## 4. Use of External Services

MdPad may load an external library from a CDN to render Mermaid diagrams.

| Service | Provider | When Used |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Mermaid library | jsDelivr CDN | Only when opening files that contain diagrams |

In this case, standard network logs (IP address, request time) may be recorded by jsDelivr's CDN servers. This is subject to jsDelivr's own privacy policy and is not controlled by MdPad. Files without diagrams generate no external network traffic whatsoever.

---

## 5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

MdPad does not use cookies, advertising trackers, or analytics tools.

---

## 6. Storage and Processing of Personal Data

All data processed by MdPad (file contents, user settings) is stored only on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers or cloud storage.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

MdPad does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 14 (or 13 in applicable jurisdictions). Since the App does not collect personal data, it is safe to use regardless of age.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Since MdPad does not collect personal data, there is no data to request deletion, modification, or access to.

To reset app settings stored on your device, you can uninstall the App or reset settings within the App.

---

## 9. Security

Since MdPad does not transmit data over the internet, your data is protected from network security threats. All files are processed only on your local device.

---

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will notify you through an app update. For significant changes, we will provide a more prominent notice.
