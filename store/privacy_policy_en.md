# mdViewer Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

## 1. Overview

mdViewer ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

## 2. Information We Collect

**mdViewer does not collect any personal information.**

The App works as follows:

- It only reads local files and folders that you explicitly select.
- All processing happens entirely on your device.
- No data is ever transmitted to external servers.
- All core features work without an internet connection.

### Local Settings Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following settings locally for your convenience:

| Setting | Storage Location | Purpose |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| Theme preference (light/dark) | Browser localStorage | Restore theme on next launch |
| Editor mode preference | Browser localStorage | Restore mode on next launch |

These values cannot identify you personally and are never transmitted externally.

## 3. External Resources

When rendering Mermaid diagrams, the App loads a JavaScript library from a CDN:

- Mermaid library: `cdn.jsdelivr.net` (only when opening files that contain diagrams)

Standard server logs (IP address, request time, etc.) may be recorded by jsDelivr's CDN servers as part of normal CDN operation. This is subject to jsDelivr's own privacy policy. Files without diagrams generate no external network traffic whatsoever.

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 14 in applicable jurisdictions).

## 5. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will notify you through an app update.
